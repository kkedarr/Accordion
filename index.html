Friend Codes or Usernames: Many games use friend codes or usernames to connect players. You can usually find your friend code or username in your game profile or settings. Share this code with friends you want to connect with, or add theirs to link up.
In-Game Friend Requests: Some games allow you to send friend requests directly through the game interface. Look for the option to add friends and follow the on-screen instructions to send requests.
Social Media Integration: Many games offer integration with social media platforms. You can often link your gaming account with your social media account to find and add friends who also play the game.
Online Lobbies or Matchmaking: For games with online multiplayer modes, you can join lobbies or use matchmaking features to find and connect with other players. This is a great way to meet new people and team up for multiplayer battles.
Clans or Guilds: Some games have clan or guild systems that allow you to join a group of players with similar interests. Joining a clan can help you find like-minded players to link up with and tackle challenges together.

How do I report a player for misconduct?
At XCanon, we take the behavior of our players very seriously and strive to maintain a safe and enjoyable gaming environment for everyone. If you encounter a player who is engaging in inappropriate behavior, such as harassment, cheating, or other violations of our community guidelines, we encourage you to report them to us immediately.
To report a player, please follow these steps:
In-Game Reporting: Most games have a built-in reporting system. Look for the option to report a player within the game menu or interface. Provide as much detail as possible about the incident, including the player's username and a description of the behavior.
Customer Support: If your game does not have an in-game reporting feature or if you prefer to report the player through a different channel, you can contact our customer support team. Visit our website and navigate to the support section, where you can find contact information or submit a ticket.
Provide Evidence: When reporting a player, it's helpful to provide any evidence you have of the inappropriate behavior, such as screenshots, chat logs, or video recordings. This will help us investigate the issue more effectively.
Privacy and Confidentiality: Rest assured that your report will be handled with the utmost privacy and confidentiality. We will investigate the matter thoroughly and take appropriate action against the player if necessary.
We thank you for helping us maintain a positive gaming community and encourage you to reach out to us if you have any questions or concerns about reporting players or our community guidelines.
